    My story

    Hello everybody!

    I am a fashion photographer Alex Rym. I have been working in this field for over 12 years. Something about interesting achievements: I photographed at the main fashion show in Paris, as well as for ZARA, VOGUE and other famous brands.

    For me, photography is an opportunity to express my vision of the world in simple things. Each photo session is a separate, unique concept with an unusual implementation.

    For many years I have been working on myself, determining what is still worth working on and constantly improving my skills. Most of the works that I present to you have been completed in the last 2 years.

    I invite you to dive into my world by looking through my photo portfolio!
